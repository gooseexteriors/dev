About Us

Goose Exteriors is a locally owned and operated pressure washing company located in Media, PA. We proudly serve Delaware County and surrounding areas with reliable and affordable exterior cleaning services.

Our mission is to help homeowners and businesses keep their properties looking fresh and well-maintained. We bring top-quality tools, detergents, and a passion for cleanliness with every job.

Whether it's your home, driveway, deck, or storefront — we clean it like it's our own.
